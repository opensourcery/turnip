Images can be uploaded and inserted into the main body of the content being created.

  1. First, click in the upload area, or click on the Browse button. Browse your computer for the desired image.

  2. Click the Upload button beside the Browse button.

  3. Once the image has been uploaded, a thumbnail of the image will appear, along with a new button labeled Send to text area. Clicking on this button will send the newly uploaded image to the main body.

  4. If the rich text editor is enabled, one can click on the image and dynamically resize it to the desired size.

  5. Image properties such as alignment and margin can be adjusted by clicking on the image button in the rich text editor toolbar.
